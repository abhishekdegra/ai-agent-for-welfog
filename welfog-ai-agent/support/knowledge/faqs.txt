What does each order status (Pending, Accepted, Hold, Shipped, Delivered) mean?
Here’s a simple explanation of Welfog’s order statuses:
Pending – Your order has been placed but not yet confirmed by Welfog.
Accepted – Welfog has confirmed your order and is preparing it for shipment.
Hold – Your order is temporarily on hold due to issues like payment verification or stock availability.
Shipped – Your order has been dispatched and is on the way to your delivery address.
Delivered – Your order has been successfully delivered to the provided address.
