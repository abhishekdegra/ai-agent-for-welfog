Return Policy:
Customer can return the product within 5 days of delivery for eligible orders.

How to return on Welfog (app or website):
1) Log in to your Welfog account.
2) Open My Orders and select the order with the item you want to return.
3) Tap Return or Replacement and upload clear photos of the product and packaging (required for wrong/damaged items).
4) After approval and pickup, refund is usually processed in 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

Refund Policy:
Refund will be processed in 5-7 business days to the original payment method after return is approved and pickup is complete.
Refund depends on order status and eligibility.
For return status on a specific order, share your Order ID in chat.
